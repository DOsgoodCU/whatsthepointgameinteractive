This is an example of an exercise to help farmers, donors, and project partners evaluate if insurance would have value.  It is one use case—many others exist.

It is intended to catalyze key discussions for investment and adaptation opportunities insurance could de-risk and incentivise, and to provide a quantitative tool for people to evaluate if it is meeting its needs.

You will be asked somequestions.  They are optional for everyone else.  If you type in your email, the many different questions can all be connected to one person.  

Enter your email (Columbia **UNI if you have one**). This is optional for most people, allowing your answers to be grouped to you. It is required for students in my class, and participants in some workshops have been instructed to.
